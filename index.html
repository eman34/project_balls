Game Rules

Welcome to the NBA Face Fusion Game! Here's how to play:

You will be presented with an image of two NBA players' faces fused together. Your task is to guess the two players and enter their names into the provided input fields.

Click the 'Submit' button to check your answers. If you're correct, you'll see a confirmation message!

If you're unsure, you can click the 'Give Up' button to reveal the answers.

Have fun and test your NBA knowledge!
